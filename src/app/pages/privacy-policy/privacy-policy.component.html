Privacy Policy

At Trading Moon, accessible from www.tradingmoon.in, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that are collected and recorded by Trading Moon and how we use it.

When you visit our website, we may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, including but not limited to:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Any message you send through the contact form

We may also collect information automatically as you navigate through the website, such as:

  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on our website

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our website
  • To improve user experience by analyzing visitor behavior
  • To respond to your messages or inquiries
  • To display personalized advertisements through Google AdSense

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Trading Moon uses cookies to enhance user experience and for tracking purposes. Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. We may use both session cookies and persistent cookies.

We use cookies to:

  • Understand and save your preferences for future visits
  • Keep track of advertisements
  • Compile aggregate data about site traffic and site interactions to offer better site experiences

4. Google AdSense and Third-Party Cookies

We may use Google AdSense to serve ads on our website. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. Google's use of the DoubleClick cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads to users based on their visit to our site and other sites on the Internet.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except as necessary to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others' rights, property, or safety.

6. Data Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously and use industry-standard methods to protect it. However, no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
