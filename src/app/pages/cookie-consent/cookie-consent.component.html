1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit websites. They are used to remember your preferences and actions, such as login information or items added to a shopping cart.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for a variety of reasons, including:

  • Analytics and Performance Cookies: We use these cookies to collect information about how visitors use our site, which helps us to improve its performance.
  • Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to display personalized ads based on your interests and browsing history.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We also allow third-party services, such as Google AdSense, to place cookies on your device to serve ads that are relevant to your interests. These third-party cookies are subject to the privacy policies of the service providers.

4. Managing Cookies

You have the option to manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of this site. To learn more about how to manage cookies, you can visit the All About Cookies website.
